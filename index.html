Strengths and weaknesses of each approach

Weaknesses: Mentally demanding, requires discipline and planning
Feynman Technique Strengths: Deepens conceptual understanding, reveals misunderstandings
Weaknesses: Time-intensive for complex topics, requires foundational knowledge
Spaced Repetition Strengths: Optimizes memory retention, efficient over time
Weaknesses: Requires consistent schedule, less effective for pure conceptual learning
